Women's Sober Living

The Road to Recovery House, a non-profit organization committed to the rehabilitation and restoration of female survivors of drug abuse. Our mission is to empower and support these women on their journey towards recovery and self-sufficiency. The Road to Recovery House offers a comprehensive range of services to assist our clients in their path to healing and personal growth. These services encompass secure housing, professional counseling, and exposure to various agencies and government initiatives tailored to address the specific needs of our clients. Our primary focus is to facilitate the development of self-esteem and coping skills among the individuals we serve. We believe that a strong sense of self-worth and the ability to effectively cope with life's challenges are fundamental to their recovery and reintegration into society. Furthermore, we are dedicated to equipping women with addictive personalities with the necessary leadership and life coping skills that will enable them to realize their full potential in life. Our goal is to provide them with the tools and resources they need to lead fulfilling, independent lives. At The Road to Recovery House, we remain committed to our mission of fostering the recovery and empowerment of female survivors of domestic abuse. We understand the importance of offering a safe and supportive environment for these individuals as they rebuild their lives..

Our Mission

1. The mission of the Road to Recovery House is centered on cultivating an environment dedicated to fostering the journey of recovery for women embarking on a transformative path.

2. Our core objectives encompass the establishment of robust, healthy, and supportive relationships, the cultivation of meaningful friendships, the implementation of structured living arrangements, and the provision of indispensable guidance essential for realizing and achieving recovery objectives, thereby enhancing the overall quality of life.

3. We are committed to assisting individuals in rebuilding their sense of responsibility, bolstering self-esteem, and nurturing self-independence. Extensive research substantiates the profound impact of prolonged immersion in a structured recovery setting, underlining its pivotal role in significantly augmenting the prospects of sustained, long-term sobriety.

Our vision is to prepare our clients to be responsible productive members of society.

We assist our clients in:

  • Finding Jobs
  • Getting to and from work
  • Making meetings
  • Snap Benefits
  • Housing Assistance
  • A structured clean environment

All while promoting life skills of:

  • Money Management
  • Cooking
  • Laundry
  • Budgeting
  • Chores
