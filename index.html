Road to Recovery

Women's Sober Living

The Road to Recovery House, a non-profit organization committed to the rehabilitation and restoration of female survivors of domestic abuse. Our mission is to empower and support these women on their journey towards recovery and self-sufficiency. The Road to Recovery House offers a comprehensive range of services to assist our clients in their path to healing and personal growth. These services encompass secure housing, professional counseling, and exposure to various agencies and government initiatives tailored to address the specific needs of our clients. Our primary focus is to facilitate the development of self-esteem and coping skills among the individuals we serve. We believe that a strong sense of self-worth and the ability to effectively cope with life's challenges are fundamental to their recovery and reintegration into society. Furthermore, we are dedicated to equipping women with addictive personalities with the necessary leadership and life coping skills that will enable them to realize their full potential in life. Our goal is to provide them with the tools and resources they need to lead fulfilling, independent lives. At The Road to Recovery House, we remain committed to our mission of fostering the recovery and empowerment of female survivors of domestic abuse. We understand the importance of offering a safe and supportive environment for these individuals as they rebuild their lives..

Our Mission

The road to recovery house is focused on providing an atmosphere of recovery for women entering a new level of recovery..developing healthy supportive relationships friendships..structured living..And the help and guidance needed to achieve their recovery goals that will improve their quality of life ..to rebuild their sense of responsibility, self esteem and self independence. Research has proven the longer and individual remains in a structured recovery environment the greater the chances they will have at maintaining a long term sobriety.

Our vision is to prepare our clients to be responsible productive members of society.

We assist our clients in:

  • Finding Jobs
  • Getting to and from work
  • Making meetings
  • Snap Benefits
  • Housing Assistance
  • A structured clean environment

All while promoting life skills of:

  • Money Management
  • Cooking
  • Laundry
  • Budgeting
  • Chores
